Privacy Policy

Effective 18 July 2026

The short version

AuraMix collects no personal data, runs no analytics, and does no tracking. Your music, your EQ settings and your listening habits stay on your device.

What the app stores, and where

Imported music files, EQ settings, presets, playlists and your Sound Profile are stored locally on your device. If you save custom presets with iCloud sync available, they are stored in your own private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit — we have no access to that data.

Network requests

AuraMix downloads its preset library and headphone-signature catalogue from our servers. These are read-only fetches of public catalogue data; no personal information, identifiers or listening history is sent with them.

Purchases

AuraMix Pro is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details.

Media library access

If you import from your Music library, AuraMix asks for media library permission and uses it solely to copy the DRM-free tracks you pick. It never scans, uploads or catalogues your library.
